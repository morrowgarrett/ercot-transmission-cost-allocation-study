# 12CP

## Formula
CP12(u,y) = (1/12) * sum_{m=1..12} L_gross(u, CP_m)

Share_12CP(u,y) = CP12(u,y) / sum_j CP12(j,y)

## Settlement interval
15-minute preferred, hourly fallback allowed.

## Annualization logic
Twelve monthly coincident peaks averaged.

## DER / BTM / storage treatment
Gross-load basis. DER and storage affect obligation only to the extent they change observed gross load.

## Export treatment
No explicit export credit.

## Cost recovery target
Full annual TCOS.

## Incentive effects
Spreads obligation across the year, reducing the leverage of a few summer curtailment windows.
